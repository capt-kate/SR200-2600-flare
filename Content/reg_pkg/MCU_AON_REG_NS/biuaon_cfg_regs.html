0x1

Sleep control disable. This disables the consolidates Q channel

controlling the global Global Clock gate. Clock will never will

gate at global level and CLK_CTRL modules are byoassed

retention and OFF mode

1: DEEP SLEEP states disabled

PPU_PCH_FORCE

0x0

This bit forces PPU Pchnnel to enter into low power modes. This

enables the Static/ Dynamic power transitions possible without

any of the p-channel activity on the devices. This is strictly

for testing only. This will not guarantee the functionality

after wake up from M52/M3.

0x0: Normal functionality.

0x1: Forces PACTIVE to PPU 0s and loops back PREQ to PACCEPT.
