Description

The CTRL register is used to cause the DWC TRNG NIST to execute one of a number of actions. The are 9 active command operations available within the DWC TRNG NIST (in addition to 0 -- NOP). CMD can only be written when the core is idle (see STAT.BUSY) unless the command is a ZEROIZE. Commands other than ZEROIZE during a non-idle condition is ignored. NOTE: This register is effectively a write-only register. Due to its volatile nature, there is no way to predict its read-back value.
