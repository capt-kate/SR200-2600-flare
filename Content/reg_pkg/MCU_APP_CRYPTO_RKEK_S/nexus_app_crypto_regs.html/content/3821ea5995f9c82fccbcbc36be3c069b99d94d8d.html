Field Descriptions

bitwidth - Operand Bitwidth

The bit length of the operands (1's base). Input zero to this field may hang the engine.

Notes:

1. The maximum value (MAX) of this field depends on the memory/

Scratch Pad DWord (32-bit) size (MemDW). In general, the

MAX=2•floor(MemDW/12) in ZMP case and

MAX=2•floor(MemDW/34) in ECP case. For example, the memory is size 512 DWords (2K Bytes). It can support up to 84 DWords (2688-bit) ZMP operand size and up to 30 DWords (960bit) ECP operand size.
