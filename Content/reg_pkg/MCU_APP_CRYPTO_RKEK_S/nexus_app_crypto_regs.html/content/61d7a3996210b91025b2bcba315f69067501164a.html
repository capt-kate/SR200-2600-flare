Field Descriptions

opmode - Operation Mode

The field determines the DES/TDES operation mode

• 0h= ECB mode

• 1h= CBC mode

engmode - Engine Select

The field selects the algorithm:

• 0h= DES

• 1h= TDES

decrypt - Decryption Indicator

The field determines the DES/TDES operation

• 0h= Encryption

• 1h= Decryption
